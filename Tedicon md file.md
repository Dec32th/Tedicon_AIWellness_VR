## 2. Project Story Progression (English)

### Phase 1: Awareness and Personal Motivation
[cite_start]As a university student, I have become acutely aware of the health crisis facing my generation, specifically the **2-30% surge in spinal diseases** and **15-20% surge in eye diseases**[cite: 14, 15]. My motivation is deeply personal; [cite_start]I recently suffered from back pain and received medical treatment, which led me to realize the critical importance of daily posture correction and stretching[cite: 10, 11].

### Phase 2: Consultation in the Health Lounge
[cite_start]The experience begins in the "Health Lounge," a calm and professional diagnostic space[cite: 39]. [cite_start]Here, the **AI Doctor** conducts a status assessment through deep conversation, analyzing my **personal health dashboard** and **comparative disease trends**[cite: 25, 27, 28].

### Phase 3: The Seamless Switch
Once the consultation is complete, I **move to a different location**, transitioning from the lounge to the studio. During this transition, the AI **changes its clothes** from a medical lab coat to athletic wear, visually signaling its role change from 'Doctor' to 'Coach.' [cite_start]All dialogue data is maintained through **data continuity** to ensure a seamless journey[cite: 47].

### Phase 4: Action in the Wellness Studio
[cite_start]I enter the "Wellness Studio," an action-oriented space designed with natural light[cite: 49, 50]. [cite_start]**VIVEAN (AI Coach)** provides real-time **motion coaching** based on the specific insights gathered during the previous lounge consultation[cite: 61, 62].

### Phase 5: Progress Tracking and Wrap-up
[cite_start]The session concludes with **performance progress tracking**[cite: 35]. This allows me to monitor my improvements and encourages the development of a sustainable wellness habit.

[cite_start]My project utilizes a **Dual-Zone** architecture to separate "Consultation" from "Action"[cite: 37, 38].
